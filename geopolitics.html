Geopolitics Kya Hai? Aasan Bhasha Mein

20 June 2025 | SukarWrites

Seedhi Bhasha Mein Geopolitics

Geopolitics matlab: Duniya ke desh apni "jagah" yani Geography ka use karke kaise Power Game khelte hain. Jaise aap Ghar mein sabse acchi kursi par baithte ho taaki TV saaf dikhe - wahi kaam Desh bade level par karte hain.

Example Se Samjho

1. **Russia-Ukraine:** Russia ko samandar tak garam paani ka rasta chahiye. Ukraine ke through NATO uska rasta rok raha tha. Result: Jung.

2. **China-India:** Himalaya ek deewar hai. Jo Himalaya control karega, wo Asia control karega. Isiliye Ladakh, Arunachal pe ladai.

3M Ka Rule Yaad Rakho

Geopolitics 3 cheez ke liye hoti hai: Money, Market, Military. Tel, Gas, Samandar ke raste jiske paas, Power uske paas.

Hum Pe Asar Kaise Padta Hai?

Petrol mehnga, Phone mehnga, Daal mehngi - sabke peeche Geopolitics hai. Russia se tel band = India mein diesel mehnga. Yehi connection hai.
