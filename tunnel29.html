Tunnel 29: Berlin Wall Ki Sabse Badi Surang

14-15 September 1962 | Berlin, Germany

Problem Kya Thi?

13 August 1961 ko Berlin Wall ban gaya. East Germany se West jaana matlab goli khana. Lakho log family se alag ho gaye.

Surang Ka Plan

Domenico Sesta aur Luigi Spina - Italy ke 2 students. Plan tha: Ek abandoned factory se West Berlin mein surang khodenge. 12 meter neeche, 140 meter lambi.

6 Mahine Ki Khudai

Roz 12 ghante khudai. Haath se. Surang thi sirf 60cm chaudi — reng ke jaana padta tha. Par ruke nahi.

Wo Raat: 14-15 Sept 1962

1 raat, 123 log. Bachche, budhe, family. Har 5 min mein ek. Agli taraf freedom thi.

Antim Jeet

Stasi ko 2 din baad pata chala. Surang seal kar di. Par tab tak 123 log azad ho chuke the. Ye thi Tunnel 29.
